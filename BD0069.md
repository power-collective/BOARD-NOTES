## bd0069_mb_v0.5
- **Date Code**: 20241210
- **MCU**: PY32Fxxx M0+ (Cortex-M0+)
- **Battery Voltage (VBAT)**: 3.88V
- **MCU Supply (VDD)**: 3.29V (regulated from VBAT)
- **SWD Connection**: Works reliably
- **Button**: PA6 (active-low, pull-up enabled)
- **Digital Sensor**: PA3 (active-high, pull-down enabled)
- **3-Way Switch**: PA2 (1-bit encoding - positions 0 and 2 are electrically identical)
  - Position 0: PA2=0 (GPIOA=0x4840)
  - Position 1: PA2=1 (GPIOA=0x4844)
  - Position 2: PA2=0 (GPIOA=0x4840)
- **ADC Channels**: CH2, CH8, CH9, CH12 (4 channels active)
  - Analog pins: PB0 (CH8), PB1 (CH9), PF2
  - Battery voltage monitor: ~0.99V stable (VBAT 3.88V ÷ 3.92 via voltage divider, referenced to VDD 3.29V)
  - Heater monitor: 3.30V at pos1 (heater ON at VDD), low at pos0/pos2 (heater OFF)
  - ADC readings by switch position:
    - Pos0: ~0.99V (battery), ~1.03V (other)
    - Pos1: ~0.99V (battery), ~1.03V (other), 3.30V (heater ON)
    - Pos2: ~0.99V (battery), ~1.02V (other)
  - Formula to calculate VBAT: VBAT = ADC_reading × (3.29V / 4095) × 3.92
- **Baseline GPIO**: GPIOA IDR=0x4840, GPIOB IDR=0x0008, GPIOF IDR=0x0004
- **Notes**: Unique 1-bit switch encoding unlike the 2-bit encoder on xwh-dm3199g board; switch controls heating mode
